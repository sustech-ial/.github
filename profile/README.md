# SUSTech Intelligent Agents Lab

The **SUSTech Intelligent Agents Lab (IAL)**, led by **Prof. Zhongrui Wang** at the **School of Microelectronics, Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech)**, focuses on **hardware-enabled AI agents**, **embodied AI**, and **hardware-software co-design**.

## Research Directions

- **Embodied AI and Intelligent Agents**
- **Vision-Language Navigation and Spatial Reasoning**
- **Memory-Centric Computing Systems**
- **Hardware-Software Co-Design for AI**
- **Emerging Memory-Based AI Accelerators**
